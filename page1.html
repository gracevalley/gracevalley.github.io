Grace Valley Public School

Grace Valley Public School, came into being in 2004 by Ikhvani Trust, is a Non-profit Educational Institution, affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi and have classes from K.G. to Secondary Level. The school aims at the holistic development of the personality of its children in an atmosphere of Islamic culture and tradition. Here every child gets ample chances to express him/herself spontaneously and creatively.
